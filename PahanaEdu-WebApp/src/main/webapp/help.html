üßæ Billing Process

  1. Go to Billing section from the menu.
  2. Select a customer (or add a new one).
  3. Search and add products to the bill by name or ID.
  4. Confirm quantities and pricing.
  5. Click "Generate Invoice" to complete the sale.
  6. System automatically updates stock and saves the transaction.
