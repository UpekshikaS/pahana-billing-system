PahanaEdu Book Shop – Help & User Guide

Getting Started

  1. Dashboard shows quick actions and system overview.
  2. Navigation menu allows access to:
    • Customers – Manage customer records.
    • Products – Add, update, and view products in stock.
    • Billing – Create invoices and process sales.
    • Reports – View summaries and inventory reports.
    • Notifications – System alerts and reminders.

Customer Management

  • View Customers: See all registered customers.
    (click on Customer ID to See the customer details)
  • View Details: Click Account No to open popup.
  • Add Customer: Use "Add Customer".
    • Email format: name@example.com
    • Phone: 10+ digits, numbers only.
  • Edit Customer: Edit
  • Delete Customer: Delete

Product & Stock Management

  • View Products: Products with stock levels shown.
    (click on Product ID to See the product details)
  • Add Product: Use "Add Product".
  • Edit Product: Update name, price, or quantity.
  • Delete Product: Remove items permanently.
  • Stock Updates: Keep inventory accurate.

Billing Process

  1. Go to Billing section.
  2. Select or add a customer.
  3. Search & add products by name or ID.
  4. Confirm quantities and pricing.
  5. Click "Generate Invoice".
  6. Stock updates automatically.

Reports & Analytics

  • Sales Reports: Filter by date.
  • Inventory Reports: Track low-stock products.

Notifications

  • All alerts from Notifications menu.
  • Reminders for low stock & pending bills.
  • Click to view related records.
